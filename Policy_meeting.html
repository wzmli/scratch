    This is a wiki for planning a policy meeting to follow on from the Glasser-organized Banff meeting in 2008. Current members are organizers David Earn, Jonathan Dushoff and David Fisman. We are thinking of adding Glasser to the wiki as an organizer emeritus.

    BIRS Organizer page

    • Our BIRS proposal was accepted for 2011. It will take place during an optimal ski week from Sunday 27 February 2011 to Friday 4 March 2011.
    • We need to:
      • finalize our initial participant list and give it to BIRS.
      • decide about DebateGraph (check out David Price's quick start version)

    Action items[edit]

    • Arrange skype tutorial on debategraph with David Price (or an in person visit if he's going to be nearby)
    • Think about streaming/videography/skyping/conferencing/including non-attendees in the event
    • Plan out structure of sessions, i.e., who will cover exactly what topic for what length of time, and what we hope the ensuing debate will be about / what elements we imagine in the debategraph that might be useful.

    History[edit]

    • Our BIRS proposal was rejected for 2010. We should revise it and resubmit using the BIRS 2011 call.
    • The official organizers will now be DE and DF, with JD working hard in the background. Judging from Referee 2's comments, there is little to be gained, and perhaps something to be lost by listing a second McMaster organizer (at least not at the time of application).
